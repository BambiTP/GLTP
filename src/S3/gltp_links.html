Rules Summary

  • Maps are released every 8 days and have assigned point values (1–3 points based on difficulty).
  • Extra points are awarded for fastest time on each map
  • Maps can be completed and submitted for speedruns any time during the season.

Replay Requirements

  • Replay link required for all point awards (completion, fastest time).
  • Fastest time replays must be submitted within 6 hours of completion.
  • No replay = No points. White names may require additional verification.

Partial Completions

  • Teams can submit a replay for partial completion if a match ends early.
  • Commissioners will issue a new map ID with a checkpoint unlocked.
  • No fastest time points awarded for these runs, only completion points.

Season Schedule

  • Signup Deadline: 4/7
  • Start & Week 1 Map Release: 4/7
  • Week 2: 4/14
  • Week 3: 4/21
  • End Date: 5/2

Game Settings

  • Gravity: Enabled
  • Respawn: 1 Second (or Instant)
  • Other settings: Follow TagPro defaults unless specified
  • Please use the presets given and only change player respawn team if desired.

Fair Play Rules

  • No botting, automation, or macros allowed.
  • All gameplay must be performed live by human players.
